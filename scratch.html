Why I Enjoy It

Pickleball is one of my favorite ways to stay active, compete, and spend time with other people. I enjoy how it combines strategy, movement, and quick decision-making.

What I Focus On

  1. Improve consistency in my game
  2. Build strong habits by practicing:
    • Serving with control
    • Clear communication with teammates
    • Quick movement at the net
  3. Have fun while competing

What I Like

I like that the game is social, fast-paced, and easy to learn while still giving plenty of room to improve.

What It Teaches

Pickleball helps build communication, patience, and adaptability because each match requires teamwork and quick adjustments.
